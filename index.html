About Me

When I was a teenager I saw an ad that started everything: my love and enthusiasm for designing and programming video games. I began learning basic programming concepts on my own through a program called Game Maker Studios. The SDK (Software Development Kit) used a markup language derived from aspects of Javascript, C++ and C#. Most of the games I made were remakes of arcade games, but GMS specifically ignited a spark inside me. That spark has grown into the raging fire for my love of software development today.


Since 2018 I have taken online courses for Computer Science, HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, Node.js, React and SQL. As a hobby, I have spent a lot of time experimenting with Unity 3D and C# for creating video games. Through multiple channels of programming, I have developed a well-rounded understanding of the world of software.


My goal is to be a part of a team collaborating on new and innovative ideas to advance the world of software as we know it.
